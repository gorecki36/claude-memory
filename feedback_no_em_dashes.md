**Zero em-dashes in any prose for Vas. Stricter than the usual "limit em-dashes" rule, this is a hard ban.**

**Why:** Em-dashes are a signature AI-writing tell. Vas wants drafts that read as human, not as model output. Global CLAUDE.md reinforces this as part of the anti-AI-writing pattern list.

**How to apply:**
- Replace em-dashes with commas, periods, colons, parentheses, or full restructuring
- Applies to en-dashes in prose too (single hyphens fine for ranges like "Apr 15-17")
- Extends to emails, essays, captions, slide content, dashboard copy, every text artifact
- Self-check before showing any draft; a missed em-dash means a redo
