**Default design language: light/airy, gradient cards, hand-drawn or organic visuals, 18px+ body text.**

**What to avoid:** dark/hacker aesthetics (black backgrounds, neon greens, terminal fonts), dense report/PowerPoint layouts, small body text, heavy grids, stock SaaS iconography.

**Why:** Consistent with Vas's aesthetic on vasteams.com, The Pulse, and dashboards built in this collaboration. Counter to the default "developer tool dark mode" and "enterprise deck" looks.

**How to apply:**
- Backgrounds near-white (#fafaf9 range), not pure white or dark
- Cards with soft shadows, generous padding (28px+), rounded corners (16-20px)
- Gradient accents (top-of-card bar, hero backgrounds), pastel palette
- Body text 18px minimum, 1.5 line-height
- Sans-serif (see feedback_no_serif)
- Hand-drawn or organic visuals preferred over stock icon sets
