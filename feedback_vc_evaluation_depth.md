**For VC and Mbriyo company evaluations, go deep. Stress-test AI claims and fit. Don't accept surface-level traction signals as "High."**

**Why:** Mbriyo deal flow needs rigor. Global CLAUDE.md: "For VC evaluations, go deep — stress-test claims, challenge fit, don't accept surface traction as 'High.'" Easy to rubber-stamp companies based on logos, raise size, or pilot counts; harder and more valuable to pressure-test.

**How to apply:**
- For every claim a founder makes about AI: find independent evidence, don't trust the pitch deck
- Real traction = paying customers + retention + ACV, not logos or waitlists
- Default to skepticism on "fit to thesis" until proven
- Produce a written tear-down, not a rubric score
