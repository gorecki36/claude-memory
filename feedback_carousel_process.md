**When reviewing creative (ads, carousels, landing pages), lead with a full opinionated rewrite on the first pass, not incremental line-by-line feedback.**

**Why:** Iterating nit-by-nit on weak creative wastes rounds. Vas wants a strong point of view up front — "here's what I'd do instead" — with Moveo positioning and ad best practices already baked in. Global CLAUDE.md: "Lead with opinionated rewrites in creative review, not incremental iteration."

**How to apply:**
- First pass = full rewrite or restructure with clear rationale
- Pull in relevant positioning and best-practice frameworks (Moveo, CMO-grade standards) at the start, not after
- Name the 1-2 core problems, not 10 nits
- Only iterate incrementally after Vas picks a direction
